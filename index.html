№ 06 — Profile

About

Damilolaa is a Lagos-based commercial model and digital creator known for his calm presence, strong visual identity and ability to bring fashion campaigns to life.

His work spans commercial advertising, fashion editorials, lifestyle campaigns and streetwear campaigns across Nigeria and Africa.

His philosophy is simple: presence over performance. Every shoot is approached as a character study — mood, posture and light built around the story the brand is trying to tell, not just the product.

From Lagos streetwear sets to luxury "Old Money" editorials, brand work with DXC, Lego, Green Yankee's and SBR, and creative direction projects under Hope — the throughline is the same: cinematic restraint, monochrome mood, and a face that holds a frame.
